# Artisan tax-exemption planning

This mode records certificate evidence, SGK confirmation status, dated annual limits, gross revenue, payouts, and bank withholding. Annual limits are data versions with sources, never permanent constants. Usage warnings occur at 50%, 75%, 90%, and 100%; reaching 100% prompts professional review for the following tax year without asserting an automatic consequence.

Withholding defaults to `UNKNOWN_PENDING_CONFIRMATION`. Expected and actual withholding remain separate, and the system does not assume Etsy net payout is the correct legal base.
